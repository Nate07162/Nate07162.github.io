Your national freight consolidation and logistics partner!

NESW offers comprehensive line-haul solutions to all main centers through our own national network for a consistent and reliable service so you can focus on the last mile.

NESW specialised services!

Break-bulk Line-haul / LCL

Not enough cargo for a full container? No problem! We offer a national consolidation service to ensure you only pay for space you need!

Dedicated Line-haul / FCL

High volumes? Great! We offer dedicated vehicles to load direct so you can get the most out of your volumes with added convenience!

Local C & D

Dont have availability or a sutible vehicle? Make use of our national footprint and extensive range of vehicles for collections and deliveries!

Airfreight

Is speed the main priority? We offer a range of Air options to ensure your urgent cargo makes it to its destination ASAP!

Contract Logistics

Want the benifits of owning your own vehicles but without the hassle? Let us handle the hassle! Speak to us about our contract logistics options.

Crating/Packaging Solutions

Getting your cargo ready for the trip is important. We offer freight preperation services to help ensure your freight can handle the journey!
